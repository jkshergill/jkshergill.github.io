The Yakuza Series by Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio

The Yakuza series (known as Like a Dragon in Japan) is a critically acclaimed video game franchise developed by Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio and published by SEGA. The games are famous for their deep storytelling, intense combat, and their faithful recreation of Japanese city life. To learn more about the latest releases, visit the official Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio website.
